Sturdy Components

Easily CDN-includable, Bootstrap-friendly web components written in HTML, CSS and JS.
Bootstrap is not required!

Hakimel Style Buttons

Hakim El Hattab developed Revealjs and founded Slides.com. When visiting his website I noticed the awesome buttons that he had there (they are also used in the Revealjs documentation).

I wanted to build those and share my solution with you.
Like everything in this project, they are easily included and follow common Bootstrap-like syntax. They are pretty much fully customizable (color, text-alignement, etc.) and come in one standard Bootstrap color.
